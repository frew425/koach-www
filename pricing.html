What's Included?

Personal Financial Strategy
Just like a winning team needs a strategy, you need one to succeed in life. We help you develop a financial strategy that will allow you to live out your life purpose. This strategy is not filled with boring graphs and charts, but rather we help put it into a story format to provide context regarding the role finance plays in your life story.
Unlimited Advisor Messaging
Whatever curve ball life throws you financially we are always here to help. Unlimited means unlimited.
Account Aggregation
Wouldn't it be nice to see how all your financial accounts are performing in one place? Now you can. You provide your financial accounts, we show you your whole financial picture in one convenient place.

Worried about providing your account information? We take the security of your account information seriously with bank level encrypition and read only access to your accounts. This means nobody, including yourself, can move money or make transactions.
Annual Credit Report
Credit Reports allow us to see your financial history and ensure we account for everything we need to in your financial strategy. And don't you want to make sure there is no suspicious activity going on with your finances? We've got your back.
Meetings
Meet with your advisor to review how you are performing in relation to your strategy. We help you tweak your strategy based on your current needs and help you come up with tasks you need to complete prior to our next meeting. We are here to encourage you to live a purposeful life but we aren't affraid to give a swift kick in the bum if you need it.

Meeting frequency is based on your monthly package.
